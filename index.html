about me

Hello, my name is Kendall Trudick and I am a 25 year old from Cleveland, OH currently living in Columbus, OH. I graduated from The Ohio State University in 2019 with a degree in chemical engineering. Since then, I worked at Anheuser-Busch for a year and a half, and now more recently I am working as a Data Analyst at Vertiv.


After realizing I wanted to make a change in my career path and take on a new challenge, I enrolled in the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at Ohio State. Through this program I have so far been exposed to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, API usage, Node.js, mySQL, Handlebars, and Express.js. I have utilized these new skills to complete numerous projects and homeworks that have helped to further my knowledge and understanding of Web Development.


I am excited for this new journey and I look forward to the doors it will open for me. Below you will get some insight into the work I have been doing to hone in on and utilize my newfound skills and knowledge.
